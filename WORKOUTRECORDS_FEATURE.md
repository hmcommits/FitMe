### 1. Global Header (Consistent across all three screens)

At the very top of each screen, the layout is split into two columns:

* **Top Left:** Fields for `date` and `day`.
* **Top Right:** Options to `add photo: (optional)` and log `weight: (optional)`.
* **Navigation Bar:** Below the header data, there are three pill-shaped buttons used to switch between workout types. Each has a specific outline color: Red (Left), Blue (Middle), and Green (Right).

### 2. Left Screen: Strength Workout (Red Tab Active)

* The first pill is labeled **`strength`** and outlined in red.
* **Inputs:** * `time(morning, evening, both) (with optional reason for each selected option in notes form.)`
* `muscle group (drop down menu)`
* `exercise (dropdown menu)`
* `no of sets`


* **Logging Grid:** Below the inputs is a horizontal grid layout for tracking sets. It is arranged in three columns (`set 1`, `set 2`, `set 3`). Under each set column, there are text labels for `weight(kg or lbs)` and `reputations` (repetitions).
* **Additions & Notes:** * An `add muscle group` button.
* Fields at the bottom for `goal for next same muscle group exercise in notes` and `notes for today's exercise`.



### 3. Middle Screen: Cardio Workout (Blue Tab Active)

* The middle pill is labeled **`cardio`** and outlined in blue.
* **Inputs:** * `time(morning, evening, both)`
* `exercise (dropdown menu)`
* `duration` (This replaces the complex sets/reps/weight grid from the strength screen, simplifying the input for cardio).


* **Additions & Notes:** * An `add exercise` button.
* The same notes section at the bottom, but tailored to cardio: `goal for next cardio exercise in notes` and `notes for today's exercise`.



### 4. Right Screen: Home Workout (Green Tab Active)

* The third pill is labeled **`home workout`** and outlined in green.
* **Everything as same as Strength section.**

At the end of the screen, for common of all three screens, there should be heading "How was the workout" with three options as "Good", "Mid", "Bad" .
